RULES


  • For every question answered correctly you move up on the money tree.
  • If a question is answered wrong you return to zero and all money not in the bank is lossed.
  • In rounds one and two the maximum amount of money you're able to win is 500,000 each
  • If the time runs out you will be taken to the next round.
  • After a round you can Bank your Money but the money tree returns to zero.
  • In round three all questions must be answered correctly to win the money in the bank.
